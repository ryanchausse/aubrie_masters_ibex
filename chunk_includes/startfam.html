Practice Session Instructions

You will see a series of conversations taking place on WhatsApp. Select a rating on the scale of 1-7 indicating how natural the conversation seems.

We are interested in your reaction to the conversations. This is not an evaluation of your English level. We simply want to know how you would react if you saw the conversation happening in the real world.

Remember, this practice session is only to permit you to become comfortable with the experiment style and technology. These responses are not recorded.
