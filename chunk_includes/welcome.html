Welcome to the experiment!

This experiment asks you to rate conversations in English on a scale of 1-7. It will take approximately 20 minutes to complete. Below is an outline of parts of the experiment.

Part One: Questionnaire and Consent to Participate (3 minutes)

Provide some background information about yourself and your level of English.

Part Two: Practice Session (<2 minutes)

Become familiar with the style of experiment, including the rating system of 1-7. These responses are not recorded.

Part Three: Experiment (approx. 10 minutes)

Provide your rating of conversations in English happening via Whatsapp/SMS.

Part Four: Survey (approx. 5 minutes)

Answer a few questions about some additional conversations in English happening via Whatsapp/SMS.

Part Five: Feedback (<2 minutes)

Give us some feedback about your experience with the survey.

Thanks for participating.
